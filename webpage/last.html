   Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What areas do you serve?
A: We currently serve the following areas: Niagra and Greater Toronto Area
Q: How do I book your services?
A: You can book our services by either by filling out a form on our website or calling
Q: What cleaning products do you use?
A: We use environmentally-friendly cleaning products that are safe for both your family and the environment.
Q: How much does your service cost?
A: Our pricing varies depending on the size of the space, the scope of the cleaning required, and any additional services requested. Please contact us for a personalized quote.
Q: Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?
A: No, we bring all the necessary cleaning supplies and equipment with us. You don't need to worry about providing anything.
Q: Are your cleaning products safe for pets?
A: Yes, our cleaning products are pet-friendly and safe for use around animals. However, if your pet has any specific sensitivities or allergies, please let us know in advance.
Q: How long will it take to clean my home/office?
A: The time required for cleaning depends on the size of the space and the level of cleaning needed. We can provide you with an estimated duration based on your specific requirements.
Q: Do you offer eco-friendly cleaning options?
A: Yes, we offer eco-friendly cleaning options using environmentally-safe products and sustainable practices. Just let us know your preference when booking our services.
Q: Can I schedule recurring cleaning appointments?
A: Yes, we offer recurring cleaning appointments on a weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly basis, depending on your needs. Contact us to set up a schedule that works for you.
Q: What safety measures do you have in place during the COVID-19 pandemic?
A: We strictly adhere to all recommended safety guidelines and protocols issued by health authorities. Our staff wears masks, gloves, and follows proper hygiene practices to ensure your safety.
